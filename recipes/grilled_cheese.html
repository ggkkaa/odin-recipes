Crispy Grilled Cheese

Description

Golden, buttery perfection. This grilled cheese sandwich is simple and wildly satisfying. Gooey in the middle, crackly on the outside. A childhood classic which you will still love.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices thick-cut sourdough or white bread
  • 2 tablespoons softened butter (or mayonnaise)
  • 2 to 3 slices sharp Cheddar cheese
  • 2 slices mozzarella cheese (optional, for extra melt)
  • 2 tablespoons caramelized onions (optional)
  • 2 slices crispy cooked bacon (optional)
  • 2 thin tomato slices (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon hot honey (optional, for a spicy-sweet kick)

Steps

  1. Heat a skillet over medium-low until hot but not smoking.
  2. Butter the outside of each bread slice generously — edge to edge, no dry spots allowed.
  3. Stack your cheese (and extras) between the slices, buttered sides facing out.
  4. Grill low and slow: Press gently and cook 3–4 minutes per side until golden brown and audibly crisp.
  5. Let it rest for a minute (if you can resist), then slice and devour.
